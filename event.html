Giving traditional essence to historial traditions through Ram Fights

Ram fighting, a traditional combat sport, has a profound impact on cultural preservation, community bonding, and heritage promotion. it encourages animal welfare practices and strengthens bonds between humans and animals. Through competitions and festivities, ram fighting promotes respect for traditions and the environment while enhancing a sense of belonging and pride within communities.
